**Effective date:** 8 September 2025

We use cookies and similar technologies. Some are essential for the site to work; others (analytics, session replay) help us improve your experience. We only set non-essential cookies after you **consent**.

---

## Categories

### Essential (required)
- **Purpose:** security, authentication, load balancing, remembering your session.
- **Legal basis:** **Legitimate interests** / **Contract**.
- **Control:** can’t be switched off (they’re required to deliver the service).

### Analytics (consent)
- **Purpose:** understand how docs, onboarding and features are used to improve Fluxzero.
- **Examples:** Google Analytics (anonymized IDs, page paths, events).
- **Control:** set only after **opt-in** via the banner. You can withdraw any time in **Cookie settings**.

### Session replay / UX (consent)
- **Purpose:** find UX issues (e.g., broken steps).
- **Examples:** Microsoft Clarity (heatmaps/replay). We **mask input fields** designed to hold secrets or personal data.
- **Control:** set only after **opt-in** via the banner. You can withdraw any time in **Cookie settings**.

*(If you use advertising or retargeting, add a separate “Advertising (consent)” section.)*

---

## Managing your choices

- When you first visit, we ask for your consent via our banner.
- You can change or withdraw consent at any time via **Cookie settings** in the footer.
- If you block all cookies in your browser, some features may not work.

## Email pixels & marketing

If you opt in to marketing emails, we may use an **open-tracking pixel** to know whether an email was opened. You can opt out of marketing at any time via the unsubscribe link in our emails or by emailing [privacy@fluxzero.io](mailto:privacy@fluxzero.io). Marketing emails are never sent without consent.
